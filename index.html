Education

I have received my BENG in the Department of Electronic and Information Engineering, Huazhong University of Science and Technology, in 2004, MSc (by research) in Department of Electronics, University of York, in 2006 and PhD in Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, Kansas State University in 2009.

Research Interest

My main research interest is evolutionary computation (evolutionary optimization, evolutionary learning, evolutionary design), meta-heuristic algorithms, data mining and their applications in real-world problems. Recently, I am focusing upon developing meta-heuristic algorithms for multiobjective (NP-hard, nonlinear, continuous or combinatorial) optimization problems with high-dimensional variables.

I have published more than 20 papers in various journals and conferences, e.g., IEEE Transaction on Evolutionary Computation, Soft Computing, Computing and Informatics，International Journal of Bioinformatics Research and Application, IEEE Congress on Evolutionary Computation (CEC2012，CEC2014), IEEE Symposium Series on Computational Intelligence (SSCI2013), ACM Proceedings in the Genetic and Evolutionary Computing Conference（GECCO2007，GECCO2008, GECCO2014）.
